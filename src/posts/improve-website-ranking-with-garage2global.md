**Step 2: Optimizing On-Page SEO**


On-page SEO allows you can control such elements directly on your site. These include:



- **Title and Meta Description:** Add title and meta description in your content.



- **Heading Tags:** Include relevant keywords and personalise your text with heading tags.

- **Placement of Keywords:** Use your keyword in our content, headings, subheadings, and conclusion naturally.
